About The Berry Patch

Renee has always had a love for fashionable handmade items and a strong desire to own her own business. In 2008 that dream became a reality when The Berry Patch was born. Mostly inspired by the two little people who call her mom and the every changing times, Renee has created handmade trendy items for not just the little people, but their fashion loving mamas.

The Berry Patch prides themselves on creating everything by hand. From cutting out each individual appliqué to hand stitching them on the highest quality garments they provide. This insures each item you receive is unique and one of a kind. Pick from one of the many designs already available for purchase or contact us if you’re looking for a more made to order custom design.
